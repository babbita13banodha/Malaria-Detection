General Symptoms of Malaria are :

Malaria Symptoms are classified into two categories :-
1.Uncomplicated Malaria
2.Severe Malaria

(I) Symptoms of Uncomplicated Malaria
  • Sensation of cold,Shivering
  • Fever,Headache,Vomiting
  • Seizers(Sometimes occurs in younger people with disease)
  • Sweats
  • Tiredness
(II) Symptoms of Severe Malaria
  • Fiver and Chill
  • Impaired Conciousness
  • Prostration
  • Deep Breathing/Respiratory Distress
  • Abnormal Bleeding/Sign of Anemia
  • Clinical Jaundice and Evidence of Vital organ dysfunction
